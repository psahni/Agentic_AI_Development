ACME Corporation — Annual Financial Report 2024

Executive Summary
ACME Corporation delivered strong results in 2024 with total revenue of 12.4 million dollars,
representing 23 percent growth over the previous year. Net profit reached 2.1 million dollars
with a net margin of 16.9 percent. The company expanded into three new markets: Singapore,
Dubai, and Toronto.

Business Segments
The Software division contributed 7.2 million dollars, up 31 percent year on year.
The Services division contributed 5.2 million dollars, up 12 percent year on year.
Software is now 58 percent of total revenue, up from 54 percent in 2023.

Risk Factors
The company faces three primary risks in 2025.
First, increased competition in the Singapore market from two new entrants.
Second, rising cloud infrastructure costs projected to increase by 18 percent.
Third, key person dependency in the engineering leadership team.

Strategic Priorities for 2025
Leadership has identified four priorities for the coming year.
One: expand the Singapore office from 8 to 25 employees.
Two: launch the enterprise tier product by Q2.
Three: reduce cloud costs through infrastructure optimisation.
Four: hire a deputy CTO to address leadership concentration risk.

Headcount
Total employees at end of 2024: 142.
Engineering: 67. Sales: 31. Operations: 28. Management: 16.
Attrition rate in 2024 was 11 percent, below industry average of 18 percent.
